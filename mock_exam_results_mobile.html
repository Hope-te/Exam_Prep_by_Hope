Which enzyme is responsible for unzipping the DNA double helix during replication?

Helicase check_circle
Polymerase

Explanation

Helicase breaks the hydrogen bonds between nitrogenous bases, allowing the strands to separate for replication. Polymerase adds new nucleotides but does not unzip the DNA.
